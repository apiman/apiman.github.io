Chat on IRC

Please feel free to join us in our IRC channel: irc.freenode.net's #apiman.

We're almost always around and this is where most of the real-time discussions about the project take place.

You can easily connect to freenode using the webchat client, or else use your favorite IRC client (recommendations below)

IRC Client Recommendations

  • Linux: XChat
  • Windows: HexChat, HydraIrc
  • Mac: Colloquy
